Biography

Kalani Sitake, the current head football coach for BYU, has led the team since 2016. A proud alumnus, Sitake played under the legendary coach LaVell Edwards and has guided the Cougars to new heights.

Early Life

Born in Tonga and raised in Laie, Hawaii, Kalani has a rich heritage of football and leadership.

Coaching Experience

  • BYU Head Coach: 2016-present
  • Oregon State: Assistant Head Coach & Defensive Coordinator
  • Utah: Linebackers & Defensive Coordinator

Personal Life

Married to Timberly Friddle, with four children. Kalani is dedicated to family and faith.

Coaching Highlights

  1. Double-digit victories in multiple seasons:
    • 11-1 in 2020
    • 10-3 in 2021
  2. First FBS head football coach of Tongan descent
  3. Polynesian Football Hall of Fame inductee (2025)
